Serving Mount Desert Island and Trenton residences and businesses with an unparalleled level of service and accommodation in waster management and disposal industry.

For commercial and construction customers, we provide and service roll off dumpsters to a much wider geographic area including all of Hancock, Washington and Penobscot Counties. If you are outside of these areas, give us a call. If we can do it, we will!

Gott’s disposal is owned by Tim and Beth Gott. The disposal division was established in 1994 in Southwest Harbor, Maine. We started this company with one truck and a handful of customers. We now operate with seven trucks, ten employees and around fifteen hundred customers!

We have a variety of disposal containers available to fit your waste hauling needs. We have the experience and expertise to recommend the correct type and size container to our customers.

We provide residential trash waste services to all of Mount Desert Island and Trenton. We provide Roll-Off construction dumpsters to a much wider area.

We are a locally owned and operated family business that is proud to ser the community.
